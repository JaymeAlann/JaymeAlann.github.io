In this version of chess, the normal implementation of capturing any piece that is attacked is changed to add some uncertainty to each action. The Attacking player must roll a dice to determine if a capture is successful. The die roll needed to capture a piece depends on the combination of the attacking piece and the defending piece shown in the table below. When the attack is successful, the attacking piece will move to the captured piece destination, if the attack is unsuccessful however; the piece will remain in its original location.
